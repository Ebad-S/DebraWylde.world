The Program

The 12-Week Transformation Program

A deeply personal, one-on-one coaching experience designed to help you gain clarity, build unshakable confidence, and create a plan for lasting change.

Coaching session placeholder

What Is the Transformation Program?

This is not group coaching, pre-recorded content, or motivational fluff. The Transformation Program is a fully personalised, one-on-one experience where you and Debra work together over 12 weeks to identify what's holding you back, clarify what you truly want, and build a concrete plan to get there.

Whether you're navigating a career crossroads, launching a new venture, or stepping into a bigger leadership role, this program meets you where you are and guides you to where you want to be.

Professional woman placeholder

Who This Program Is For

  • Women in leadership or transitioning into leadership roles
  • Business owners seeking clarity on growth, strategy, or direction
  • Professionals feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or misaligned with their current path
  • Women ready to invest in themselves and commit to real change
  • Those who want honest, personalised guidance - not cookie-cutter advice

Who This Program Is Not For

This program requires commitment and honesty. It's not the right fit for everyone, and that's okay.

Looking for Quick Fixes

If you want overnight results without doing the inner work, this isn't the right program for you.

Not Ready to Be Honest

Transformation requires vulnerability and truthfulness - with yourself and with your coach.

Seeking Generic Motivation

This is deep, structured coaching - not surface-level cheerleading or pre-packaged pep talks.

Structure

How the 12 Weeks Unfold

Each phase builds on the last, creating momentum and measurable progress week by week.

1

Weeks 1–3: Foundation

Assess where you are now. Clarify your goals, identify patterns, and build the self-awareness that fuels everything that follows.

2

Weeks 4–7: Breakthrough

Challenge limiting beliefs, develop new strategies, and start taking bold action towards your vision with Debra's guidance.

3

Weeks 8–10: Integration

Cement new habits, refine your approach, and navigate real-world challenges with confidence and support.

4

Weeks 11–12: Launch

Finalise your roadmap, prepare for sustained progress, and step into your next chapter fully equipped and confident.
